Kaveri Kitchens is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving authentic Multi-cuisine-Restaurant for over 4 years and It is a group of "Yellow Rice Food LLP". Our passion for food and love of family are the cornerstone of our philosophy and the foundation of our menu.

  • At Kaveri Kitchens, we believe that every meal should be a celebration of life and love. That's why we use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients in our dishes, and why we make every dish with care and attention to detail.
  • Our menu is inspired by classic recipes that have been passed down from generation to generation, and every bite is a taste of the warm, welcoming family table.
  • Behind Kaveri Kitchens a dedicated team of chefs, managers, and staff members who are passionate about food and committed to providing our customers with the best possible dining experience.

When you step into Kaveri Kitchens, you'll be transported to a cozy, Italian trattoria. Our warm, inviting atmosphere and friendly service will make you feel right at home, and our menu of classic dishes will make your taste buds sing.

Why Choose Kaveri Kitchens?

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, or just want a delicious meal in good company, Kaveri Kitchens is the perfect destination. We hope to see you soon and share a meal with you at Kaveri Kitchens!
